Percussion Part Scheduler

Generate fair, balanced daily percussion part assignments for your ensembles. Everything runs in this browser tab — nothing you type here is sent to a server. Export a file to save your work.

Your data stays here. This page keeps everything in your browser's memory and (as a safety net) in this browser's local storage, so a refresh won't lose your work. Nothing is ever uploaded anywhere. To back up your setup or move it to another computer, use Save / Load to download or open a file.

Instruments

Define each instrument, whether it's pitched or unpitched, how many students can play it per day, and its role. Core instruments are assigned first and are required each day (up to the daily limit). Optional instruments are assigned next, if there's room. Leftover students get filled in from your Fill-role instruments — a pitched one if they still need pitched days to hit their ratio, otherwise an unpitched one. Make sure you have at least one active Pitched instrument and one active Unpitched instrument marked as Fill (any names you like — "Bells" and "Pad" are just the defaults).

Students

Add each student, the ensemble they belong to, and check any days they will be absent / unavailable. The scheduler builds one schedule per ensemble. You can also import a roster from a CSV file — a names-only file works fine too; any missing ensemble or absence info just defaults to blank/available and you can fill it in here afterward. Download the Excel template for a fillable version with dropdown menus and instructions.

Generate Schedule

Runs up to 1,000 randomized trial schedules per ensemble and keeps the best-scoring one — fewer repeats of the same instrument, and each student's pitched/unpitched ratio matched to how many days they're available.

Save / Load

Your instrument list, roster, and most recently generated schedules can be saved to a file on your computer, and loaded back later or on another computer. This file is the only copy of your data outside this browser tab — keep it somewhere safe.

How this works

1. Set up your Instruments — mark each Pitched or Unpitched, give it a daily limit, and assign it a role.
2. Add your Students with their ensemble and weekly availability.
3. Go to Generate, pick an ensemble (or generate all at once), and review the schedule.
4. Use Save / Load to back up your setup or bring it to another computer.

Nothing is stored on a server. If you close this tab without saving, your data is gone — the local-storage safety net will restore your last session in this browser, but it won't help you on a different device.

Runs entirely in your browser. No accounts, no server-side storage, no tracking of student data.
